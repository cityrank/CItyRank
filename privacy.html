CityRank - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1st, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect only minimal personal information, specifically the country selected by the user during setup. This data is stored securely with Apple’s CloudKit service and is used solely to provide a localized experience within the app.

We do not collect location data or other personal information. All data remains within the Apple ecosystem and is not shared with any third parties.

2. Use of Collected Information

The collected country information is used solely to position the user on the map and to display relevant information based on the user’s selected country. This data helps improve the app’s functionality by providing a more localized and personalized experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any user information to third parties unless required by law.

4. Data Security

We prioritize data security and take precautions to protect any collected information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

Non-personal data, if collected, is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, after which it is securely deleted.

6. AI-Generated Content and User-Submitted Events

CityRank provides AI-generated city insights and allows certain users to submit events. We are not responsible for the accuracy, reliability, or availability of AI suggestions or user-submitted events. Users should verify details independently.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or other means. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for updates. Changes are effective upon posting.

8. Your Consent

By using CityRank, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
